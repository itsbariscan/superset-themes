# Hangar — moodboard

**Concept:** A working warehouse at the end of a shift. Concrete floors, steel cantilever shelving, diesel-scented air. Functional, not decorative.

**Anchor references:** Polished concrete (warm grey), safety-orange high-visibility paint, exit-sign red, diesel/sodium-yellow under industrial lights. Think Donald Judd warehouse, FedEx terminal, BMW Welt back-of-house.

**Anchor colors (sampled):**
- Concrete dark: `#1c1d1f` (cool slate, not black)
- Concrete dust / forklift line: `#e8e6df` (off-white with warmth)
- Safety orange: `#ff6b1a` (the "look up" color)
- Diesel yellow: `#e6c34c` (caution stripe)
- Exit-sign red: `#d62828` (only for the truly destructive)
- Steel grey: `#3a3d42` (cantilever frame)

**Mood checklist:**
- Dark, but warm — concrete is not asphalt.
- Primary is the safety orange. It's the only "loud" color.
- Greens absent on purpose — no plant life in this space.
- Reds reserved for stop/exit/destructive — not used for charts or syntax.
- Foreground has slight warm cast (concrete dust) so the page feels lived-in.

**What this theme avoids:** purple, magenta, pastel anything, true blacks.

**Type:** dark

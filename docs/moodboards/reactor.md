# Reactor — moodboard

**Concept:** A cooling pool inside a research reactor. Cherenkov-blue glow on graphite walls. Uranium-yellow caution signage. The hum of moderated neutrons.

**Anchor references:** Spent-fuel pools, control-room CRTs, radiation-trefoil yellow, EBR-II photographs. Cold, technical, slightly intimidating.

**Anchor colors:**
- Graphite dark: `#0d1216` (cool dark, deep moderator block)
- Foreground: `#cce6f5` (pool reflection — pale icy blue-white)
- Cherenkov blue: `#2cb4e8` (primary — the glow)
- Uranium yellow: `#e8c635` (caution — secondary/highlight)
- Steel grey: `#1a2128` (card)
- Hot zone red: `#e8412f` (destructive — radiation alert)
- Containment border: `#26323a`

**Mood:** Cool, precise, a little dangerous. Where Hangar/Ferro are warm-industrial, Reactor is cold-industrial. Greens absent on purpose.

**Type:** dark

# Riso — moodboard

**Concept:** A risograph print, fresh off the drum. Fluorescent pink and soybean yellow misregister-overlapped on a cream sheet. Zine aesthetic, art-school basement.

**Anchor references:** Risograph prints, screenprinted zines, "The Counter-Press" portfolio, Mattie Bennett's risograph studio. Specifically the FLUORESCENT_PINK + YELLOW + FEDERAL_BLUE inks.

**Anchor colors:**
- Riso cream: `#f4ecd6` (smooth ground — the paper)
- Foreground: `#1a1812` (riso black — soft, slightly muddy)
- Fluorescent pink: `#ff5290` (primary — the loud one)
- Soybean yellow: `#f8c93b` (highlight — the warm one)
- Federal blue: `#1f4f8a` (accent — the cool one)
- Mint green: `#7fcfa6` (chart — the cool surprise)
- Misregister border: `#d8c8a0`

**Mood:** Light theme. Fluorescent confidence. The colors should feel slightly off-register, slightly handmade. Print-shop, not pixel-perfect.

**What this avoids:** Pure greys. Pure black. Pure white. The whole point is the imperfection of ink on absorbent paper.

**Type:** light

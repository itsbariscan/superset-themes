# Twilight Run — moodboard

**Concept:** The moment after the streetlights come on but before the sky goes black. Pre-dawn navy meets sodium-vapor orange. A bridge over a freeway, a runner exhaling steam.

**Anchor references:** Sodium streetlamps under indigo sky, Edward Hopper's nightscapes, Wong Kar-wai's *Chungking Express* — neon over wet pavement but cooler.

**Anchor colors:**
- Pre-dawn navy: `#0e1b2a` (deeper than Tokyo Night, less indigo)
- Foreground: `#dde3ee` (cold steam-white)
- Sodium orange: `#ff8a3d` (primary — the streetlamp)
- Headlight white-yellow: `#f5e493` (highlight)
- Brake-light red: `#e8526a` (destructive)
- Concrete shadow: `#1c2638` (card)
- Wet asphalt: `#0a1320` (sidebar)

**Mood:** Dark, cinematic, *temporal* — captures a *moment* rather than a place. Different from Tokyo Night (which is whole-city neon). Sodium orange is the load-bearing distinguishing color.

**Type:** dark

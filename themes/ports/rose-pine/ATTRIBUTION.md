# Rosé Pine attribution

**Original theme:** Rosé Pine by the Rosé Pine team
**Source:** https://rosepinetheme.com / https://github.com/rose-pine
**License:** MIT (see [LICENSE](./LICENSE))

This is a port of the Rosé Pine "Main" variant (the darkest of three: Main, Moon, Dawn) to the superset.sh theme schema. Hex values match the canonical Rosé Pine palette spec at https://rosepinetheme.com/palette. Iris (`#c4a7e7`) is mapped to `primary` consistent with Rosé Pine's documented brand mapping. Moon and Dawn variants may be added in a future release.
